Movie101

This is a movie review website. It will store information about a variety of movies. The aspects of the movie review data will include information such as the release date, director, the distributing company and actors in the movie. Movies will also have a list of comments and reviews which are contributed by registered users. Users can have accounts, which can be used to write reviews, comments or store a list of their favourite movies.

There will be three types of users in our database: non-registered users who can only view and query information, registered users who can write comments and reviews, and administrators have special access to an Admin Center where they can add or remove resources from the website. Once posted, a review can be viewed by all users on the website. A user may edit their own reviews after posting.
